1. Var and let scoping rules are different. var : function scope let: block scope
	var can reassign, redeclare and mutate. Var is hoisted to the top of their parent element. Can be 	accessed even before they are declared.
	let can reassign and mutate but CANNOT redeclare

2. var: function scope const : block scope. 
	var can reassign redeclare and mutate.Var is hoisted to the top of their parent element. Can be 	accessed even before they are declared.
	const can reassign and mutate but cannot be redeclared. 

3. let: can reassign and mutate but cannot redeclare
    const: cannot reassign or redeclare

4. Variables declared with var are hoisted to the top of their parent element which allows the variable to be 	accessed before they are declared.
